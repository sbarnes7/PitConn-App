OPTICAL PLATE





     



The OPA is an open optical system that permits basic visualization of sample structure during rheological experiments, revealing important insights about material behavior under flow. An open platform with a borosilicate glass plate provides a transparent optical path through which the sample can be viewed directly. This enhances the understanding of a range of materials, especially suspensions and emulsions. The accessory is easy to use and install, accommodates diverse optical systems, and offers accurate temperature control over a wide range for flow visualization and microscopy.
