ADVANCED PELTIER PLATE





     



The new Advanced Peltier Plate combines ultimate flexibility with exceptional temperature performance in a single Peltier Plate temperature system designed to cover the widest range of applications. The unique Quick Change Plate system provides the ability to easily attach lower plates of different materials and surface finishes, disposable plates for testing curing materials, and an Immersion Cup for characterizing materials in a fluid environment.

  • Smart SwapTM technology
  • Wide temperature range: -40 °C to 200 °C
  • Accurate temperature control: ± 0.1 °C
  • Plates and cones up to 50 mm in diameter
  • Robust hardened finish
  • Quick Change Plates provide flexibility for modifying lower geometry surface
    • Stainless steel, Anodized Aluminum and Titanium plates
    • Smooth, Sandblasted and Crosshatched plates
    • Disposable plates available for curing materials
  • Fully accessorized
    • Extremely efficient Solvent Trap with built-in Purge Cover
    • Immersion Cup
    • Camera Viewer option
  • Open platform to easily adapt custom-made lower geometries
