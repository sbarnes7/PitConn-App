Discovery Hybrid Rheometer





    View Instrument Page 


The Discovery Hybrid Rheometer is available in three models to meet your specific needs of sensitivity and capability. From the world’s most sensitive rotational rheometer – the HR-3 – to the general purpose HR-1, every Discovery Hybrid Rheometer is built on a common core of the TA Instruments advanced drag cup motor and second generation magnetic thrust bearing. Find out how the Discovery Hybrid Rheometer will improve your rheological characterization by making better measurements over a wider range of conditions with less time in the laboratory.

Features and Benefits:

  • Optical Encoder Dual Reader
    This patent pending technology provides unimaginable resolution of better than 2 nanoradians while enhancing phase resolution and eliminating drift. (Model HR-3 only)
  • Normal Force Rebalance Transducer
    The DHR combines popular force rebalance transducer, or FRT, technology with the world’s most advanced single-head Rheometer for zero compliance highly accurate normal force measurements up to 50N.
  • Second Generation Magnetic Thrust Bearing
    Patented design with improved low torque performance and mapping stability. With less than 70% of the friction of air-bearings, it can measure down to 0.5 nNm of torque. Inherently more robust and not susceptible to contamination compared to air-bearings. Patent # 7,137, 290 & 7,017,393
  • New True Position Sensor
    Patent-pending technology provides true gap accuracy via a high resolution linear position sensor that makes real-time gap corrections for movement due to thermal expansion. Works with all smart SWAP™ geometries and environmental systems.
  • Advanced Drag Cup Motor
    The DHR incorporates our redesigned & patented drag cup motor, with digital current control for more stable torque output and minimal drift. The DHR motor provides extremely smooth acceleration, the fastest step strain and step rate response, and keeps inertia, temperature, and friction to an absolute minimum. Patent # 6,798,099
  • Active Temperature Control
    Patented technology enables wireless temperature measurements across an air gap in non-contact systems. With ATC, the actual upper plate temperature is known rather than inferred, making real-time control of both plates possible. The benefits are faster temperature response, true temperature ramp capability, and elimination of complex calibration procedures and offset tables. Patent # 6,931,915
  • Smart Swap Geometrics
    Features patented Smart Swap™ geometries. When attached, the information is automatically read and the software is configured with appropriate parameters (type, dimension, material).
    Patent # 6,952,950
  • Smart Swap Temperature Systems and Accessories
    Smart SWAP™ options are attached to the instrument on its unique magnetic base and automatically detected and configured for operation in less than 10 seconds.
  • Intuitive Touch Keypad
    A robust, intuitive keypad inspires simple interaction by placing the most important rheometer functions at your fingertips. Available functions include: gap zero, trim gap, go to gap, raise and lower head, start and stop tests. The keypad employs a rugged, sealed design that resists even the most aggressive solvents, providing worry-free operation in any environment.
  • Single Piece Aluminum - Casting and Linear Ball Slide
    New single-piece aluminum casting and rugged linear ball slide configuration reduces torsional and axial compliance by 60% over traditional designs. A micro stepper motor and linear optical encoder ensure precision positioning of the geometry with a resolution of 0.02 microns. The open design provides ease of access and ample space for sample loading and trimming.
  • Color Display
    Displays a variety of information to the test station to facilitate sample loading and viewing of real-time information during experiments.
