X3 DSC

The World’s Finest line of Differential Scanning Calorimeters.





       



The new Discovery X3 Differential Scanning Calorimeter features a multi-sample cell with patented Fusion Cell and Tzero™ technologies that delivers high quality heat flow data for up to three samples simultaneously. The new instrument provides the same industry-leading performance found on TA’s popular Discovery DSC. Beyond the fact that the X3 DSC can easily produce results for statistical evaluations three times faster, the baseline quality and sensitivity of the cell provide users the opportunity to compare various formulations or competitive materials side-by-side under the exact same test conditions. For pharmaceutical, materials science, polymer processing and many other applications, the X3 DSC multi-sample cell can run verification standards and samples simultaneously to ensure the calibration accuracy.

  • Productivity – triple your output for QC or statistical evaluation.
  • Comparisons – different formulations or competitive materials, side-by-side, under the same conditions.
  • Verify – instrument calibration checked simultaneous to measuring valuable pharmaceutical samples.
  • Batch Processing – Trios software easily handles analysis, reporting and control charting of large data sets.
