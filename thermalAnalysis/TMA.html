Penetration & Hemispherical Softening Temperature (Ts)


Determination The penetration fixture was used to test polycarbonate /acrylonitrile-butadienestyrene (PC/ABS), an amorphous thermoplastic blend, at a controlled heating rate of 5 °C/min and a constant force of 0.2 N. Conditions outlined in ASTM E1545 and ISO 11359 were followed in the assignment of the softening temperature/ glass transition by penetration. The softening points are easily detected as a negative deflection in dimension change, and individual softening points were observed for each component of this blend.
