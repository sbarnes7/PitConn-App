BIODYNAMIC 5200

Multi-specimen Mechanical Stimulation Bioreactors



     



ElectroForce® multi-specimen BioDynamic® test instruments can be used for a variety of tissues and biomaterials, and allow reproducible mechanical stimulation, for statistical characterization of multiple specimens. With multi-mover capabilities for tension/compression loading and dynamic (pulsatile) flow stimulation, multi-specimen BioDynamic instruments from TA ElectroForce open up new research possibilities for advanced tissue engineering and biomaterials research

Features and Benefits:

  • Versatile chambers adaptable to blood vessels, cardiac muscle, bone, cartilage, meniscus, spinal discs, ligaments, tendons, and skin.
  • Multi-chamber system with independent media loops and controlled loading of all samples to measure statistical variability
  • Multi-axial loading capabilities and characterization of mechanical properties

BioDynamic 5210

Instrument with compression platens for stimulation and characterization of bone, cartilage, intervertebral discs, and other disc-shaped tissues and biomaterials. Optional tensile grips for loading of samples like ligaments, tendons, and skin.

BioDynamic 5270

Instrument for simultaneous or independent tension/compression and pulsatile stimulation and characterization of a variety of samples like cartilage, tendons, and vascular tissues.

BioDynamic 4-Chamber Pulsatile

Instrument for pulsatile stimulation and characterization of vascular tissues.
